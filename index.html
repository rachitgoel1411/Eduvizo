About Us



Eduvizo is an IITians' initiative to help you assess various career options and choose the right one for YOU in an extremely convenient and cost-effective manner. Eduvizo is incubated at the IIT Mandi Catalyst, a Central Government funded state of the art incubator. We have created a web-based platform where students can connect with mentors (industry and academic experts from tier 1 colleges and companies) in a one-on-one interaction to solve their queries regarding career, college admissions, subject related questions, exam preparation and many more. Till date Eduvizo has helped more than 250 students in their career related queries. We pride in our team and their dedication towards helping you which is evident by our user rating of 9.4.
